About Us

Northwest Pavers & Patio

Northwest Pavers & Patio is a family-owned hardscape company based in Pierce County, WA. We started with a simple idea — do the work right, treat people fairly, and stand behind every project we complete. That approach hasn't changed.

We specialize in paver patios, driveways, retaining walls, and outdoor living spaces. Every job is handled by our own crew, not subcontractors, so you always know who's showing up and who's accountable for the finished product.

As a local business, we work in the same neighborhoods we live in. That means your project matters to us beyond the invoice. We take pride in work that holds up through Pacific Northwest winters and still looks great years down the road.

Whether it's a small backyard patio or a full property hardscape, we bring the same attention to detail to every job. No shortcuts, no surprises — just honest work from a team that takes it personally.
