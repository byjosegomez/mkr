About Mathew kendrick Renovations

MKR Design & Construction was founded in 2017 by Matt Kendrick with a clear mission: deliver high-quality remodeling built on trust, craftsmanship, and strong client relationships. Born and raised in Oklahoma City, Matt began his career as a kitchen designer at a cabinet and countertop supply company, where he developed a deep technical understanding of the trades while working closely with homeowners, suppliers, and skilled trade partners. That hands-on foundation continues to shape the way MKR approaches every project today.

With over 20 years of industry experience, Matt brings a practical, detail-driven mindset to remodeling. He understands that successful projects aren’t just about design and construction — they’re about communication, coordination, and accountability at every stage. From planning through completion, MKR prioritizes clarity, responsiveness, and quality execution.

Now based in Tacoma, Matt lives with his wife Nicole and their two children and remains actively involved in the local community. As a family-owned business, MKR takes pride in serving the same neighborhoods where its team lives and works. That local connection shows in the care taken with every home and every client relationship.

What sets MKR apart is its commitment to craftsmanship and consistency. With in-house tradesmen and trusted partners, the team maintains high standards across every phase of construction. The focus is always on long-term quality, client satisfaction, and building spaces that genuinely improve how people live in their homes.

At MKR, remodeling isn’t just a service — it’s a partnership built on trust, experience, and results that stand the test of time.
