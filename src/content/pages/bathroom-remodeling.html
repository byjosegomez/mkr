The Upgrades Homeowners Love

  • Heated Floors

    Step out of the shower or bathtub onto warm, comfortable floors. Heated flooring adds a touch of luxury and makes your bathroom feel spa-like all year round.

  • Freestanding Bathtubs

    Create a centerpiece for relaxation with a freestanding bathtub. This elegant upgrade enhances your bathroom’s style while providing a perfect soak after a long day.

  • Walk-In Showers

    Open, modern walk-in showers with frameless glass and premium fixtures combine function and style. Customize with rain showerheads, body sprays, and bench seating.

  • Custom Vanities

    Upgrade your storage and style with a custom vanity designed to fit your space. Includes thoughtful features like soft-close drawers, built-in organizers, and designer countertops.

  • Smart Mirrors & Lighting

    Enhance functionality and ambiance with smart mirrors, LED lighting, and dimmers. Perfect for morning routines or creating a calming evening environment.

  • Luxury Bathroom Storage

    Optimize space with built-in cabinets, linen closets, or medicine cabinets. A well-organized bathroom keeps clutter out of sight and adds lasting value.
